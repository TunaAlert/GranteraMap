The calendar of Grantera

Grantera's calendar is based on its experience with magic, separated into 4 ages: Pre, Asin, Pureza and Sabi.

Pre

The Pre age covers everything before the start of the Asin age and runs backwards, meaning year 1 Pre is the last year of this age, preceded by year 2 Pre. In this age, magic was not discovered yet.

Asin

The Asin age, named after the discoverer of magic Asin, starts with magic's discovery in year 1 Asin. The Asin age lasted 523 years.

Pureza

The Pureza age is coined by the Pureza belief which dictates a pure life free of magic. The Pureza belief started in the year 511 Asin and spread through all of Grantera in just 15 years. The Pureza age starts with the ban of magic in the Northern-Granteran Alliance. The Pureza age lasted 217 years.

Sabi

The Sabi age (from sabidura = wisdom) is coined by the embrace of magic in the name of wisdom. In the year 212 Pureza, a large revolution in today's Ontigoron fought against the Pureza belief and slowly pushed it out of existance. With the start of the Sabi age, the revolution disbanded the Northern-Granteran Alliance and with it the ban on magic. North and central Grantera grew to embrace magic and its potential while the south held onto the Pureza belief much longer and only accepted rudamentary magic into their lifestyle.

The present year is around 312 Sabi.
