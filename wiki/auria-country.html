The empire Auria

The countries official name is The Great Empire of Auria. It started off as a city in the mountains that took over more and more of the surrounding regions, grew its military might and became the largest country in the world.

Auria started off as a mining city in the mountains (Auria City) which focused largely on mining gold, which is where it gets its name (lat. aurum = gold). This lead to the city being known for its wealth and many people moved there. The cities government, which was an oligarchy at the time, grew hungry for more power and started building an army intended to take over nearby regions, which promptly happened.

At first the city ensured its control over nearby villages who accepted their fate and joined the cities administration without any resistance. The first real fight was over the city of Besando Rios, which auria occupied from the far, south-eastern side near the river, blocking off important trade routes in the process. After an occupation of 122 days the city gave in and accepted to join Auria's administration on the condition that one elected representative may join Auria's governing body. Auria accepted that deal, but the elected representative was always overshadowed by the other oligarchs and not taken seriously.

Auria used the resources of the regions it took over to strengthen and grow its army to ensure their conquest turns out even more fruitful than it has up until that point. It split its Army into two fronts, one conquering along the river towards Porto Bosquo, the other along a mountain pass towards Cielo Verde. Porto Bosquo was taken over in just 60 days as Auria controlled most of their trade partners for metallic resources and trade routes. The region controlled by Cielo Verde took 544 days to conquor as Auria was restricted by a mountain pass and Cielo Verde maintained their trade routes. However, Auria managed to capture the city of Cielo Verde and with that the rest of the region under their administration.

While conquering Cielo Verde, Auria also started taking over the regions down the river on the eastern front, which they were very successful at, taking over everything until Sangradelta, where they tried to expand into what is now known as the Southern Plains which to their surprise was very unsuccessful as the individual regions bonded together and built an army of their own that used boobytraps and ambushes to cut off small groups of Aurias army, which they could then overrun using a large amount of people.

With the capture of Cielo Verde's region, Auria was no longer landlocked and started building a marine fleet which they used to occupy Corrianta both from the sea and the north-eastern land. Corrianta, which belonged to a country called Rios Montaños which expected this attack to occur, so Corrianta was not easy to capture. The war with Rios Montaños over Corrianta lasted about 355 days, at which point Auria had managed to cut off supply routes well enough that they could enter the city. It took almost 4 years to capture the rest of Rios Montaños, at which point Auria had also started a war with Pantera Blanca over Boca Mare.

The fight over Boca Mare went by rather quickly, taking only 116 days, at which point Auria expanded their front northward to Deseaderra, whose eastern side was captured in 203 days as supply routes by Pantera Blanca were weak. The western side was a lot better supplied and the fight over West-Deseaderra lasted until the end of the war 556 days later, before which Auria captured Tierrablanca.
